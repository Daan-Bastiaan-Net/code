Answers

  • Sing in on print.hz.nl using your school account and follow the instructions on the site
  • Sing into on of the pronters using your student card and press Scanning-scan. Follow the instructions on the printer
  • Visit the webshop via HZ protal and add my item to your shopping cart. To finalize your purchase follow the instructions on the webshop
  • Inform your teachers and try the courses online
  • Go to the selfservice portal and make a reservation to book a projectroom
  • Free parking is possible on the car park of PZEM at the poelendaelesingel 10 in Middelburg. Due ti construction work please keep in mind its not possible to reach HZ via the tunnel
